An introduction to sound and music

What is sound?

Sound is a pressurized wave, travelling through a transmission medium such as a gas, liquid or solid

Our ears interpret the frequency of sound waves as pitch

What are notes?

A note is a soundwave played at a certain frequency

A doubling in frequency yields the same note, just an octave higher

What is harmony?

Harmony is the combination of notes played simultaneously

Harmonious notes can be described as a mathematical relationship against a root note
